Welcome to Pig Game! Roll the dice to start your turn.

How to Play:

  • Roll the dice to accumulate points in your Turn Score.
  • You can roll as many times as you want to increase your score.
  • Click Hold to save your turn score to your Total Score and pass the turn to the other player.
  • Warning: If you roll a 1, your current Turn Score is lost (set to 0), added directly to your opponent's Total Score, and turn passes immediately!
  • The first player to reach 100 total points wins the game!
